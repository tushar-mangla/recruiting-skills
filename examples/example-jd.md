## Software Project Manager — B2B Marketplace Platform

**Company**: TradeFlow (Series A startup, 80 employees, Prague HQ)
**Department**: Product & Engineering
**Reports to**: VP of Engineering
**Location**: Hybrid (2 days/week in Prague office) or remote within EU (CET ±2h)

### About Us

TradeFlow is building the Shopify for B2B wholesale. Our marketplace connects suppliers with retailers across Europe, simplifying how businesses trade. We raised €12M Series A in 2023 and serve 15,000+ active users with 30% year-over-year growth.

### The Role

We're looking for a Software Project Manager to lead delivery for our core product team. You'll own the roadmap execution for a 12-person cross-functional team (4 backend devs, 3 frontend devs, 2 QA, 1 designer, 1 product owner) during a pivotal phase: migrating from monolith to microservices while shipping major new features.

### Responsibilities

- Own end-to-end project delivery for the product team using Agile/Scrum methodology
- Run sprint planning, daily standups, retrospectives, and sprint reviews
- Manage stakeholder expectations across engineering, product, sales, and leadership
- Identify risks early and develop mitigation strategies
- Coordinate the monolith-to-microservices migration alongside feature development
- Track and report on delivery metrics (velocity, cycle time, quality)
- Support the team in removing blockers and improving processes
- Contribute to quarterly roadmap planning with product and engineering leaders

### Requirements

**Must-have:**
- 5+ years of experience in project management, with at least 3 years managing software/tech projects
- Hands-on experience running Agile/Scrum ceremonies with development teams
- Strong stakeholder management skills — you can translate between business and engineering
- Understanding of the software development lifecycle (CI/CD, code reviews, deployment processes)

**Important:**
- Experience leading cross-functional teams of 8+ people
- Background in SaaS or B2B product companies
- Budget and resource management experience
- Risk management and ability to rescue troubled projects

**Nice-to-have:**
- PMP, Prince2, or Certified Scrum Master certification
- E-commerce or marketplace industry experience
- Experience managing remote or distributed teams
- Involvement in large-scale migrations or platform scaling initiatives

### What We Offer

- Salary: €55,000 - €70,000 annual gross
- Hybrid work: office in Prague center (Tue/Thu) or full remote within EU
- 25 paid vacation days
- Meal vouchers, MultiSport card, home office budget (€500/year)
- Learning budget: €1,000/year for courses and conferences
- A stable, collaborative team with low turnover (2+ years average tenure)
