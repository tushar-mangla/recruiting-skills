# Requirement Analysis: Software Project Manager

## 1. Technical Stack
- No coding required, but must understand software development lifecycle
- Team uses React (frontend), Python/Django (backend), PostgreSQL, AWS
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions) is a plus

## 2. Core Competences
- Project management and delivery leadership
- Stakeholder management and communication
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Resource planning and allocation
- Agile methodology facilitation (Scrum)

## 3. Seniority
- Senior level (5+ years in project management)
- 3+ years managing software/tech projects specifically

## 4. Job Fulfillment
- Hybrid: 2 days/week in office (Tuesday, Thursday)
- Core hours 10am-4pm, flexible otherwise

## 5. Specific Background / Industry Experience
- SaaS or B2B product company experience preferred
- E-commerce or marketplace experience is a strong plus

## 6. Certifications Required
- PMP or Prince2 preferred but not mandatory
- Certified Scrum Master (CSM) is a plus

## 7. Sourcing Locations
- Office in Prague, Czech Republic
- Remote-friendly within EU timezone (CET +/- 2 hours)
- Not sourcing from outside EU due to contract/legal constraints

## 8. Personality & Culture Fit
- Collaborative, not command-and-control
- Comfortable with ambiguity in a growing company
- Strong communicator who can translate between technical and business teams
- **TO ASK: What specific traits does the hiring manager value most in their direct reports?**

## 9. Hiring Process
- Round 1: Recruiter screen (30 min)
- Round 2: Hiring manager interview (45 min)
- Round 3: Case study presentation + team meet (90 min)
- Decision within 1 week of final round

## 10. Administration
- Recruitment contract signed: Yes, signed 2024-01-15

## 11. Screening Questions
1. Tell me about the largest cross-functional project you've delivered. What was the team size and timeline?
2. How do you handle scope creep when stakeholders push for additional features mid-sprint?
3. What project management tools have you used, and which do you prefer?
4. Describe a project that failed or was significantly delayed. What did you learn?
5. What's your experience with Agile/Scrum ceremonies?

## 12. Relocation
- Relocation within EU is supported
- No visa sponsorship (EU work permit required)
- Small relocation package available (up to €3,000)

## 13. Contract
- Full-time employment
- Expected start: within 2-3 months
- Direct contract with the company
- 25 paid holiday days
- Benefits: meal vouchers, MultiSport card, home office budget

## 14. Compensation
- €55,000 - €70,000 annual gross
- Monthly salary

## 15. The Position
- Dream job for: a PM who wants to own delivery end-to-end at a growing product company, not just push tickets
- Red flags: candidates who only managed waterfall projects, no Agile experience
- No excluded companies

## 16. Project
- Building a B2B marketplace connecting suppliers with retailers
- 15,000+ active users, growing 30% YoY
- Interesting challenge: migrating from monolith to microservices while maintaining feature velocity
- Short-term: deliver Q2 product roadmap (3 major features)
- Long-term: scale the platform for international expansion
- **TO ASK: What's the biggest delivery risk the hiring manager sees in the next 6 months?**

## 17. Team
- Team of 12: 4 backend devs, 3 frontend devs, 2 QA, 1 designer, 1 product owner + this PM role
- Team has been together for 2+ years, low turnover
- Use Jira, Confluence, Slack, GitHub
- 2-week sprints with standard Scrum ceremonies
- **TO ASK: Why do current team members say they like working here?**

## 18. Company
- Series A startup, 80 employees, founded 2019
- "We're building the Shopify for B2B wholesale" — simplifying how suppliers and retailers trade
- Raised €12M Series A in 2023 from reputable EU VCs
- Growing fast but sustainable — not burning cash recklessly
