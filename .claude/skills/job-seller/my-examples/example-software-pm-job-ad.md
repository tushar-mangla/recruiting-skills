# Example: Software Project Manager — Rewritten Job Ad

## "So What?" Reframing

| Feature | Candidate Benefit |
|---------|-------------------|
| Series A startup, 80 people | Big enough to be stable, small enough that your work visibly moves the needle |
| Hybrid 2 days/week in Prague | Flexibility of remote with the energy of in-person collaboration |
| Team of 12 cross-functional | You own the full delivery — no layers of bureaucracy between you and the product |
| Monolith-to-microservices migration | A genuine technical leadership challenge, not just ticket management |
| B2B marketplace, 15K+ users | Real users, real scale, real impact on how businesses trade |
| €12M Series A from EU VCs | Funded runway — you can focus on building, not worrying about survival |

## Rewritten Job Post

### Own the Delivery of a Product Used by 15,000+ Businesses

We're building the Shopify for B2B wholesale — a marketplace that simplifies how suppliers and retailers trade across Europe. We raised €12M Series A, have 80 people, and are growing 30% year-over-year.

**The challenge**: We need a Project Manager who can lead a 12-person cross-functional team through a pivotal phase — migrating our monolith to microservices while shipping 3 major features this quarter. This isn't ticket-pushing. You'll own end-to-end delivery and work directly with engineering, product, and design.

**What makes this interesting**:
- **Real ownership**: You run the sprints, manage the stakeholders, and make the calls. No layers of PMs above you.
- **Technical complexity**: Monolith-to-microservices isn't just an architecture change — it's an organizational one. You'll navigate both.
- **International expansion ahead**: The next phase is scaling the platform across European markets. You'll shape how we grow.
- **A team that stays**: Low turnover, 2+ years together. They care about the product and respect good process.

**What you bring**:
- 5+ years managing software project delivery (Agile/Scrum environments)
- Track record of shipping products with cross-functional teams of 8+
- You understand the SDLC — you don't code, but you speak the language
- Stakeholder management is your superpower — you translate between business and engineering naturally

**The details**: Hybrid in Prague (Tue/Thu in office) or remote within EU timezone. €55K-€70K. 25 vacation days, meal vouchers, MultiSport, home office budget.

## Outreach Hooks

1. **Impact angle**: "Your team at [Company] ships great product — curious if you'd be interested in owning delivery end-to-end for a platform used by 15K+ businesses?"
2. **Challenge angle**: "We're mid-migration from monolith to microservices while shipping 3 major features this quarter — looking for a PM who thrives in that kind of complexity."
3. **Growth angle**: "We're about to expand across Europe and need a PM to shape how we scale — thought of you given your experience at [Company]."
4. **Team angle**: "The team's been together 2+ years with almost no turnover — they're looking for a PM who respects good process and earns trust through delivery."
