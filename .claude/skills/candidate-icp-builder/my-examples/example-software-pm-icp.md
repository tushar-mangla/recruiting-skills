# ICP: Software Project Manager — B2B SaaS Marketplace

## Role Context
- **Title**: Software Project Manager
- **Seniority**: Senior (5+ years)
- **Location**: Hybrid in Prague, CZ or remote within EU (CET ±2h)
- **Compensation**: €55,000 - €70,000 annual
- **Team**: 12-person cross-functional product team

## Scoring Rubric

| # | Criterion | Tier | Weight | How to Evaluate |
|---|-----------|------|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | Project delivery track record | Must-have | 3 | Look for 3+ completed software projects with measurable outcomes (on time, on budget, shipped features) |
| 2 | Stakeholder management | Must-have | 3 | Evidence of managing competing priorities across business and engineering teams. Look for C-suite or VP-level interactions |
| 3 | Agile/Scrum experience | Must-have | 3 | Has run sprints, retros, planning sessions. Look for Scrum Master or Agile Coach experience in profile |
| 4 | Software development understanding | Must-have | 3 | Can speak to SDLC, CI/CD, code reviews. Doesn't need to code but must understand the process |
| 5 | Cross-functional team leadership | Important | 2 | Has led teams with devs, QA, design, product. Look for team sizes of 8+ |
| 6 | Budget and resource management | Important | 2 | Has managed project budgets or team allocation. Look for P&L or budget mentions |
| 7 | SaaS / B2B product experience | Important | 2 | Worked at a SaaS or B2B company. Understands subscription metrics, product-led growth |
| 8 | Risk management | Important | 2 | Can identify, communicate, and mitigate project risks. Look for examples of rescuing troubled projects |
| 9 | PMP or Prince2 certification | Nice-to-have | 1 | Listed in certifications section |
| 10 | E-commerce / marketplace domain | Nice-to-have | 1 | Worked on marketplace, e-commerce, or platform products |
| 11 | Remote/distributed team management | Nice-to-have | 1 | Has managed teams across timezones or remote-first environments |
| 12 | Migration/scaling experience | Nice-to-have | 1 | Has been part of a monolith-to-microservices migration or major scaling initiative |

## Scoring Summary
- **Must-have criteria**: 4 (max 36 points)
- **Important criteria**: 4 (max 24 points)
- **Nice-to-have criteria**: 4 (max 12 points)
- **Maximum possible score**: 72 points
