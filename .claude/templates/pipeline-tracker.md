## How to Use

1. Copy the template that fits your workflow (markdown for notes, CSV for spreadsheets)
2. Update stages as candidates progress through your pipeline
3. Review source effectiveness weekly — double down on channels with high response rates
4. After the role is filled, save your source effectiveness data in a playbook (`playbooks/`) so you can reference it next time you hire for a similar role
